Game Description

Objective of Connect 4: Become the first player to connect four of the same colored circles in sequence (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).
How to Play: Click on a white circle on the board, The circle will drop to the lowest point of which three is not another colored circle resting. Player one and player two will alternate turns each clicking an open circle to drow their colored circle. Only one drop is permitted per turn and it can be dropped into any of the columns. Once a player has connected 4 in a sequence, they have won the game. Tne game can also end in a stalemate if the board becomes full of pieces and no more discs can fit in the gameboard. There is a special button called "SUPER POWER", this will reverse the order of all disks according to row. This must be done prior to placing a colored disk in a slot. This button can only be pressed once-per-game per player. Most of all, have fun, good luck.
